The 5 whys to identify the Root-Cause, (ex. Lets increase our sales.)

Why Cant we increase our sales? Because we cant open newer stores.
Why cant we open new stores? Because we are not allowed to.
Why are we not allowed to open new stores? Because regulations dont allow us.
Why regulations dont allow us to open new stores? Because we dont have the permit.
Why dont we have the permit? Because we dont know how to file.

Thats it? The solution is simple:
Research the filing, submit the permit, follow regulations, open new stores, and Bam! You sell more, earn more.
